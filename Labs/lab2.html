Cameron Walker


In this lab we build a time-series map using CARTO to display road collision events in Auckland between 2000 and 2004. This data was obtained from the New Zealand Transport Agency (NZTA) Crash Abalysis System. It was abstracted and released as open data for HACKAKL, Auckland's first civic hackathon in 2014. We obtained a copy from Koordinates.com.
